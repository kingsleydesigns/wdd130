Nigeria

Nigeria, situated in West Africa, is a vibrant and diverse nation renowned for its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and significant economic potential. With a population exceeding 200 million, it stands as the most populous country on the African continent. Nigeria boasts a complex tapestry of ethnic groups, languages, and traditions, contributing to its dynamic cultural mosaic. Economically, it is a powerhouse in Africa, fueled by its abundant natural resources such as oil, agriculture, and minerals.
